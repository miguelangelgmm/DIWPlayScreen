Iron Man 1

Iron Man is an action-packed science fiction superhero movie, directed by Jon Favreau and starring Robert Downey Jr. in the lead role of Tony Stark / Iron Man. The movie tells the story of a billionaire industrialist named Tony Stark, who is kidnapped by a group of terrorists and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, Tony builds a high-tech suit that enables him to escape from his captors.

Spiderman 1

Spider-Man is a superhero movie based on the popular Marvel Comics character, directed by Sam Raimi and starring Tobey Maguire in the lead role of Peter Parker/Spider-Man. The movie tells the story of the shy high school student Peter Parker, who after being bitten by a radioactive spider, acquires superhuman powers that transform him into the iconic superhero Spider-Man.

Uncharted

Uncharted is an action-adventure movie based on the popular video game series of the same name, directed by Ruben Fleischer and starring Tom Holland in the lead role of Nathan Drake. The movie follows the adventures of treasure hunter Nathan Drake, who teams up with his mentor, the experienced treasure hunter Victor Sullivan, to find the legendary lost treasure of a pirate.
